Seattle Mariners Farm System

The Rebuild

The Seattle Mariners are in the midst of rebuild. They are looking to build a talented and sustainable organization through statistics and player development on and off the field. Over the past year, Jerry Dipoto has rebuilt the organization that has a multitude of young talent and a bright future ahead of them.

2020 Season Goals

The 2020 Season will be the next big step in the rebuild process. Many young players will have the opportunity to make their mark on the big league club. Pitchers like Justus Sheffield, Justin Dunn & Marco Gonzales will have the chance to prove themselves as building blocks for the future.

2021 Season Goals

The 2021 Season hopes to a competitive one. Rising stars like Jarred Kelenic & Julio Rodriguez will get a chance to shine on the biggest stage. The hope is to be able to compete for a division title and at the least a wild card spot.

2022 Season Goals

The 2022 Season is planning to be the year that the Mariners establish themselves as a Top 5 team in the leauge. All the young players should be on the Major League team by now and pushing this new wave of baseball in Seattle to the postseason.
